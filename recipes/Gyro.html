Gyro

Most of us don't have a vertical, rotating spit at home, but that doesn't mean you shouldn't be able to enjoy delicious gyros whenever you want! Make juicy, tender, seasoned lamb gyro meat at home with just a bread loaf pan!

This can be served with a side of fries, a salad, or any traditional greek side dish you may have on hand. I recommend some nice hummus and some stuffed grape leaves.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds ground lamb
  • 1/2 cup greek seasoning
  • 1 large egg
  • 12 pita breads, warmed
  • 1 head iceberg lettuce
  • 5 roma tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 medium red onion
  • 1/2 cup tzatziki sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 275 degrees F.
  2. Spray large loaf pan with cooking spray
  3. Combine lamb, greek Seasoning, and egg in bowl.
  4. Mix until ingredient are very well combined.
  5. Firmly press mixture into prepared loaf pan and cover with foil.
  6. Cook in preheated oven until center reads at least 160 degrees F.
  7. Remove from oven and let rest until all grease on top is absorbed.
  8. Remove meat from pan and cut into thin slices
  9. Load meat into pitas with lettuce, tomatoes, onion, and sauce.
  10. Serve with sides and enjoy!
